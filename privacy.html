MultiplicX — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 29, 2026

MultiplicX does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app works entirely offline.

All user profiles, scores, and preferences are stored locally on your device and are never sent to any server or third party.

The app does not use analytics, advertising, tracking, or any third-party services.

The app does not require an internet connection.
